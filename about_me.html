About Me

Welcome to my photography page!

I am currently a first year graduate student at UCLA, majoring in aerospace engineering. Photography became my hobby in senior year of high school, after I took a digital photography class. That was the start of my journey as a photo enthusiast.

I am an active member of the Photography Club at UCLA. I also worked as an event and portrait photographer for UCLA Community Programs Office from 2013 to 2015, and from 2017 until now. I Have learned SO MUCH from friends I met in the club and from my job. Thanks to everyone who helped me along the way!
